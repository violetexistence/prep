# Crawling Torso
## Creature 2
==small== ==undead== ==unholy==
**Perception** +6; lifesense 30 feet, tremorsense (imprecise) 30 feet.
**Languages** Common, Shoanti (can't speak any language)
**Skills** Athletics +8, Diplomacy +6

**HP** 40

---

**Speed** Climb 15 feet
**Melee** `[one-action]` grasping hand +10 (agile), Damage 1d4+4 plus Grasp of the Hopeless.

**Grasp of the Hopeless** `[one-action]` This ability functions as [[Grab]], but the crawling torso moves with the creature grabbed instead of holding it in place. A creature grabbed this way is [[Encumbered]] but never retrained, even on critical hit. If the target is larger than Medium, it is unaffected.

**Insupportable Misery** `[one-action]` (emotion, fear, mental, visual) **Requirements** The crawling torso has a creature grabbed or restrained. **Effect** The crawling torso pleads wordlessly with the creature to destroy it. The creature must attempt a DC 18 Will save.
	**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
	**Success** The creature is [[Frightened]] 1.
	**Failure** The creature is [[Frightened]] 2.
	**Critical Failure** The creature is [[Frightened]] 3.
