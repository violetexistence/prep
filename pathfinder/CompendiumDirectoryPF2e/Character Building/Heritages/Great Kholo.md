# Great Kholo

### 






You're an imposing, powerful kholo, with tawny fur and brown spots on your hide. You gain 10 Hit Points from your ancestry instead of 8 and gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Athletics checks to [[Actions/Reposition|Reposition]], [[Actions/Shove|Shove]], or [[Actions/Trip|Trip]].

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
