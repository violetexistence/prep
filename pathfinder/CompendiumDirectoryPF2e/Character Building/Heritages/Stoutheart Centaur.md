# Stoutheart Centaur

### 






You're a hardy centaur, firm of stance and strong of heart. You gain 10 Hit Points from your ancestry instead of 8 and gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Acrobatics checks to [[Actions/Balance|Balance]] and to your Reflex DC to avoid being tripped.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
