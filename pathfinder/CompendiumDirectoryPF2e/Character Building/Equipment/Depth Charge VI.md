# Depth Charge VI
## Item 16
### [[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** ammo; 
**Price** 1500 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** other

**Ammunition** any

* * *

Carved with stylized images of water or aquatic life, depth charges that are fired underwater or at a submerged target function with their normal range increments and can hit no matter their normal damage type. This ammunition explodes if it hits a target underwater, dealing 13d6 bludgeoning damage in a 20-foot burst `DC 36 Fortitude check` according to its type. This burst doesn't extend out of the water.
