# Goring Horn
## Item 7
### [[graft]][[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 320 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** implanted

A beast's horn or horns have been grafted onto your skull. You gain a horn unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage. This horn is in the brawling group and has the forceful and shove traits.
