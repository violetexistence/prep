# Frenzied Quintessence
## Item 14
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[whetstone]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 900 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

Contained within this small iron-bound glass sphere is a raging whirlwind of colors, each fighting for prominence. After a successful Strike with a weapon enhanced by _frenzied quintessence_, you are [[Conditions/Quickened|Quickened]] for 1 round. You can use the additional action only to Stride toward a foe or Strike.
