# Slashing Claws
## Item 3
### [[graft]][[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 50 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** implanted

Sharp claws have been grafted to your hands or feet, perhaps extending from your knuckles or the tips of your toes. You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals 1d4 slashing damage. These claws are in the brawling group and have the agile and finesse traits.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
