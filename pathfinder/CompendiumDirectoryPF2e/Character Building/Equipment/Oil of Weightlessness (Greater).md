# Oil of Weightlessness (Greater)
## Item 6
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** oil; 
**Price** 36 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)
