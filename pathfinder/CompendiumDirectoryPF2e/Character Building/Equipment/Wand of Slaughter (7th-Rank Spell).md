# Wand of Slaughter (7th-Rank Spell)
## Item 16
### [[magical]][[void]][[wand]]


**Price** 10000 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This polished black wand has a green gem at the tip, and anyone who looks into it sees a reflection of a grinning skull.

* * *

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Frequency** once per day, plus overcharge

**Effect** You cast [[Spells/Execute|Execute]] at 7th-rank. If the spell slays a living target, the corpse releases grim energy in a 20-foot emanation, dealing 14 void damage.

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply a casting of _execute_ at 7th-rank.
