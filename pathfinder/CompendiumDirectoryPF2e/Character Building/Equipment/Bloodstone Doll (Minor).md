**Category** other;

**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:r` (concentrate, manipulate)

**Trigger** A creature within 60 feet casts a summon spell

**Requirements** You have a free hand.

This small doll has been carved from a fragment of Bloodstone. When you Activate a bloodstone doll in response to another's magic, you Interact to take it in hand and hold it up toward the triggering creature. Attempt a counteract check against the triggering spell with a counteract modifier of +9 and a counteract rank of 2. If the spell would be counteracted, the bloodstone doll instead influences the summoned creature. You gain control of the summoned creature and can dictate its 2 actions for that turn. Your bloodstone doll shatters when the summon spell ends
