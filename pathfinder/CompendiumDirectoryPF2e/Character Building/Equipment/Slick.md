# Slick
## Item 5
### [[magical]]


**Price** 45 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** etched-onto-armor

This property makes armor slippery, as though it were coated with a thin film of oil. You gain a +1 item bonus to Acrobatics checks to Escape and Squeeze.
