# Preserved Moonflower
## Item 7
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[alchemical]][[consumable]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 60 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

**Access** Tian Xia origin

* * *

Though these preserved vegetables aren't actual moonflowers, they're made using a moonflower-distilled vinegar.

When consumed, tendrils sprout from your veins and curl around you, granting you a +2 status bonus to saves against void effects for 10 minutes and reducing your wounded condition by up to 2.

[[Equipment Effects/Effect_ Preserved Moonflower|Effect: Preserved Moonflower]]

Eating more than one preserved moonflower dish in a day doesn't grant further benefits and makes you [[Conditions/Drained|Drained 1]].

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Tian Xia Character Guide*
