# Acid Spitter
## Item 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[acid]][[clockwork]][[consumable]][[mechanical]][[snare]][[trap]]

**Category** snare; 
**Price** 15 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This tin clockwork lizard is activated when a creature moves adjacent to it, at which point it spits out a glob of acid. The target must succeed at a `DC 20 Reflex check` saving throw or take 3d6 acid damage.

* * *

**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.

**Success** The creature takes full damage.

**Failure** The creature takes full damage and 5 persistent acid damage.

**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage and 10 persistent acid damage.

*Source: Pathfinder Guns & Gears*
