# Bagpipes of Turmoil
## Item 4
### [[coda]][[occult]][[staff]]


**Price** 90 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

While those who appreciate bagpipes may like the sound of this gray reed and black leather instrument, its real purpose is to sow turmoil against a performer's enemies, spreading discord with each note. While playing the bagpipes, you gain a +1 item bonus to Performance checks and to Intimidation checks made to [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]].

* * *

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Effect** You expend a number of charges from this instrument to cast a spell from its list.

*   **Cantrip** [[Spells/Daze|Daze]]
*   **1st** [[Spells/Bane|Bane]]

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed ranks of all listed spells.
