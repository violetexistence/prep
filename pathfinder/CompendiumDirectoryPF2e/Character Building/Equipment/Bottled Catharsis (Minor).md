# Bottled Catharsis (Minor)
## Item 2
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]][[elixir]][[healing]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

This drink unlocks a flood of emotions that helps reset your mental state. When you drink this elixir, the elixir attempts to counteract at 1st-rank and has a +6 counteract modifier each effect on you that has the emotion trait or is inflicting the [[Conditions/Stupefied|Stupefied]] condition on you, using the listed counteract rank and modifier.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
