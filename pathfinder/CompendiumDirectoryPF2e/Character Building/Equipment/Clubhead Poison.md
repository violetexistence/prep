# Clubhead Poison
## Item 12
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]][[injury]][[poison]]

**Category** poison; 
**Price** 340 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:2` (manipulate)

* * *

This poison is named for the strain of fungi from which it's distilled. Hallucinations assail the victim's mind, causing them to see imaginary foes.

**Saving Throw** `DC 32 Fortitude check`

**Maximum Duration** 6 rounds

**Stage 1** 3d8 poison damage, [[Conditions/Off-Guard|Off-Guard]], and can't take reactions (1 round)

**Stage 2** 4d8 poison damage, off-guard, can't take reactions, [[Conditions/Stunned|Stunned 1]] (1 round)

**Stage 3** 5d8 poison damage, off-guard, can't take reactions, and stunned 1 (1 round)
