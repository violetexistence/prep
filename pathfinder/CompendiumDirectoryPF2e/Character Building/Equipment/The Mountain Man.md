# The Mountain Man
## Item 20
### [unique](unique "Unique Rarity Trait")[[artifact]][[harrow-court]][[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 0 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** carried

**Suit** Shield (Con)

**Alignment** chaotic neutral

* * *

_The Mountain Man_ represents creatures with incredible physical power. As long as you have _The Mountain Man_ invested, it grants you a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks to [[Actions/Force Open|Force Open]], [[Actions/Grapple|Grapple]], [[Actions/Shove|Shove]], or [[Actions/Trip|Trip]]. This increases to a +3 item bonus if you are at least 17th level.

**Activate** `pf2:2` command, Interact

**Frequency** once per day

* * *

**Effect** You bring the might of the mountain into your body. You cast 2nd-rank or 4th-rank [[Spells/Enlarge|Enlarge]] upon yourself. If you're at least 15th level, the duration increases to 1 hour.

*Source: Pathfinder #190: The Choosing*
