# Elemental Ammunition (Lesser, Fire)
## Item 1
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]][[fire]][[splash]]

**Category** ammo; 
**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Ammunition** any

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

When activated, the reservoir of alchemical reagents in elemental ammunition atomizes on impact, dealing 1 persistent fire damage to the target and 1 splash fire damage in addition to the damage the attack normally deals. Each damage type requires a different formula, and the ammunition gains a trait matching the damage type.

*Source: Pathfinder Treasure Vault (Remastered)*
