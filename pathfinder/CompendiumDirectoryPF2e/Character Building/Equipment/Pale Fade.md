# Pale Fade
## Item 19
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]][[injury]][[poison]]

**Category** poison; 
**Price** 6000 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:2` (manipulate)

* * *

Pale fade is a white ointment with a sharp, earthy scent. The poison rapidly desiccates flesh, which then crumbles and forms a cloud of pallid dust. If the victim is [[Conditions/Concealed|Concealed]] by this poison, then the dust cloud also conceals other creatures from the victim.

**Saving Throw** `DC 42 Fortitude check`

**Maximum Duration** 6 rounds

**Stage 1** 10d6 poison damage and [[Conditions/Drained|Drained 1]] (1 round)

**Stage 2** 12d6 poison damage, drained 1, and concealed (1 round)

**Stage 3** 15d6 poison damage, drained 1, and concealed (1 round)

*Source: Pathfinder Treasure Vault (Remastered)*
