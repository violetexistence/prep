# Chilled Fire Noodles
## Item 1
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:3` (manipulate)

**Access** Tian Xia origin

* * *

These cool noodles are served with dark fermented sauces and vinegars before finally being tossed with spicy chili oil.

When you consume the noodles, you temporarily ignore the -1 status penalty to AC and saving throws caused by the [[Conditions/Fatigued|Fatigued]] condition for 1 hour.
