# Oil of Animation
## Item 12
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** oil; 
**Price** 330 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-two-hands

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

You can rub this bronze-colored oil onto a melee weapon to grant it the _[[Equipment/Animated|Animated]]_ rune. Once you fail a flat check for the weapon, causing it to fall, this effect ends.
