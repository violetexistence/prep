# Potion of Swimming (Greater)
## Item 11
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[potion]]

**Category** potion; 
**Price** 250 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)
