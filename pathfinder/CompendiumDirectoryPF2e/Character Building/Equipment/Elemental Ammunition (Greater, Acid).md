# Elemental Ammunition (Greater, Acid)
## Item 11
### [[acid]][[alchemical]][[consumable]][[splash]]

**Category** ammo; 
**Price** 210 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Ammunition** any

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

When activated, the reservoir of alchemical reagents in elemental ammunition atomizes on impact, dealing 3d4 persistent acid damage to the target and 3 splash acid damage in addition to the damage the attack normally deals. Each damage type requires a different formula, and the ammunition gains a trait matching the damage type.
