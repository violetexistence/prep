# Rooting (Major)
## Item 15
### [[magical]][[plant]][[wood]]


**Price** 6500 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** etched-onto-melee-weapon

Small roots grow along the surface of the weapon, clinging tightly to its contours. On a critical hit with the weapon, roots grow from the target. It's [[Conditions/Immobilized|Immobilized]] for 1 round ([[Actions/Escape|Escape]] DC 34) and [[Conditions/Clumsy|Clumsy 1]] for as long as the immobilization lasts.
