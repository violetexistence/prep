Lodestone bombs hold reactive ionized minerals preserved in a dormant state until broken. The bomb grants a +2 item bonus to attack rolls and deals 3d4 force damage and 2 force splash damage. In addition, a target made of metal, wearing metal armor, or using metal weapons takes 2d4 persistent force and is [[Conditions/Clumsy|Clumsy 1]] and [[Conditions/Enfeebled|Enfeebled 1]] while taking the persistent damage. The persistent damage can last up to 1 minute.
