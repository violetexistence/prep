# Healer's Gel (Lesser)
## Item 5
### [[catalyst]][[consumable]][[magical]]

**Category** catalyst; 
**Price** 25 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** Cast a Spell

* * *

An astringent aroma from rare succulent plants wafts off these soothing cloth patches. Adding this material component to a [[Spells/Heal|Heal]] spell bestows an additional 5 temporary Hit Points to one target healed by the spell. These temporary Hit Points last for 1 minute.
