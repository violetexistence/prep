# Potion of Grounding
## Item 10
### [[consumable]][[electricity]][[magical]][[potion]]

**Category** potion; 
**Price** 185 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

* * *

Sparks flash within this amber syrup. Drinking this potion turns you into a living lightning rod for 1 minute, drawing nearby electricity to strike you instead of allies. You gain the [[Actions/Divert Lightning|Divert Lightning]] reaction while the effect lasts.
