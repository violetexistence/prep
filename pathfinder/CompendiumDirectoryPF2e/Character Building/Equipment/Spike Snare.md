# Spike Snare
## Item 1
### [[consumable]][[mechanical]][[snare]][[trap]]

**Category** snare; 
**Price** 3 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This basic snare consists of hidden spikes that rely on a creature's momentum to lacerate or potentially impale it as it enters the snare's square, dealing 2d8 piercing damage. The creature must attempt a `DC 17 Reflex check` saving throw.
