# Bronze Bull Pendant
## Item 2
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 7 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:1` (concentrate)

* * *

This pendant is forged from grainy steel and depicts a snorting bull's face. The pendant must be attached to the chest area or on a shoulder guard. When you activate the pendant, attempt an Athletics check to [[Actions/Shove|Shove]] with a +1 item bonus to check. Increase the distance you Shove your target to 10 feet on a success or 20 feet on a critical success.
