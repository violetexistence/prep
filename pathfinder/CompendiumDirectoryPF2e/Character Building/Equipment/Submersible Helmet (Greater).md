# Submersible Helmet (Greater)
## Item 13
### [[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 2700 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** wornheadwear

A _greater submersible helmet_ is a streamlined sallet with a visor and a flanged rear. While wearing the helmet with the visor down, you can see, hear, and speak clearly underwater. You also have a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks to Swim.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:3` (concentrate, manipulate)

**Frequency** once per day

**Effect** You can breathe underwater for 8 hours. During this time, you have a swim Speed equal to your land Speed.

*Source: Pathfinder Treasure Vault (Remastered)*
