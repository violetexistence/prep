# Eye of Apprehension
## Item 12
### [[consumable]][[fortune]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 400 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-armor

**Activate** `pf2:0` (concentrate)

**Trigger** You are about to roll Perception for initiative but haven't rolled yet
