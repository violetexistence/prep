# Anklets of Alacrity
## Item 17
### [[apex]][[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 15000 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** wornanklets

These gem-studded golden anklets give you a +3 item bonus to Acrobatics checks. When you invest the anklets, you either increase your Dexterity score by 2 or increase it to 18, whichever would give you a higher score.

* * *

**Activate** `pf2:1` Interact

**Frequency** once per day

* * *

**Effect** You click the anklets together, gaining a +20-foot status bonus to all your Speeds and the effects of [[Spells/Water Walk|Water Walk]] for the next 10 minutes.
