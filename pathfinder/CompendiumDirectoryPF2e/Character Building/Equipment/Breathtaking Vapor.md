# Breathtaking Vapor
## Item 17
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[alchemical]][[consumable]][[inhaled]][[poison]]

**Category** poison; 
**Price** 3000 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

This colorless mist has a mild, waxy scent that precedes acute shortness of breath. Creatures that don't need to breathe can still take the poison's damage but are immune to its other effects.

**Saving Throw** `DC 38 Fortitude check`

**Onset** 1 round

**Maximum Duration** 6 rounds

**Stage 1** 6d6 poison damage, [[Conditions/Drained|Drained 1]], and can't breathe

**Stage 2** 8d6 poison damage, [[Conditions/Drained|Drained 2]], and reduce remaining air by 1 additional round

**Stage 3** 10d6 poison damage, [[Conditions/Drained|Drained 3]], and reduce remaining air by 2 additional rounds
