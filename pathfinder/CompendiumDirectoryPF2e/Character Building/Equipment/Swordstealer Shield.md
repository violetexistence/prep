# Swordstealer Shield

### [[deflecting-slashing]]


**Price** 6 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

This specialized steel shield features several wide metal hooks along its surface. These hooks help catch swords and other blades, reducing the impact of their incoming attacks.
