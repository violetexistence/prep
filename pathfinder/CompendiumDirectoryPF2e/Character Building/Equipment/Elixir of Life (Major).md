# Elixir of Life (Major)
## Item 15
### [[alchemical]][[consumable]][[elixir]][[healing]]

**Category** elixir; 
**Price** 1300 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

Elixirs of life accelerate a living creature's natural healing processes and immune system. Upon drinking this elixir, you regain 8d6+21 healing Hit Points and gain +3 item bonus to saving throws against diseases and poisons for 10 minutes.
