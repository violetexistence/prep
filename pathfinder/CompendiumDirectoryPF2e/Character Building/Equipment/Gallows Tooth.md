# Gallows Tooth
## Item 8
### [[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 100 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-weapon

**Activate** `pf2:1` (manipulate)

* * *

This grisly molar hangs from a cord threaded through a hole just above its dried, exposed root. When you activate this talisman, make a melee Strike against an adjacent creature. That creature is [[Conditions/Off-Guard|Off-Guard]] against the Strike and until the end of your turn.
