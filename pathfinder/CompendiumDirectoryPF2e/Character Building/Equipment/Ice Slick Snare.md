# Ice Slick Snare
## Item 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[cold]][[consumable]][[mechanical]][[snare]][[trap]]

**Category** snare; 
**Price** 40 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

Using a [[Equipment/Frost Vial (Moderate)|Frost Vial (Moderate)]] as a catalyst, this snare spills out over the ground when triggered to create a slippery patch of ice on the ground. When a creature triggers this snare, it takes 2d6 cold damage and must attempt a `DC 22 Reflex check` saving throw.
