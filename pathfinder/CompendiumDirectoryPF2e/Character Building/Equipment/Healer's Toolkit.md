# Healer's Toolkit

### 


**Price** 5 gp; 
**Bulk** 1; **Size** med
**Usage** other

This kit of bandages, herbs, and suturing tools is necessary for Medicine checks to [[Actions/Administer First Aid|Administer First Aid]], [[Actions/Treat Disease|Treat Disease]], [[Actions/Treat Poison|Treat Poison]], or [[Actions/Treat Wounds|Treat Wounds]]. If you wear your healer's toolkit, you can draw and replace its tools as part of the action that uses them.
