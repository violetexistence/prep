# Hail of Arrows Snare
## Item 16
### [[consumable]][[mechanical]][[snare]][[trap]]

**Category** snare; 
**Price** 1500 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

When a creature enters the snare's square, it releases hundreds upon hundreds of carefully prepared arrows, blanketing a 20-foot radius around the snare's square with massive arrow fire that deals 18d6 piercing damage. Creatures in the area must attempt a `DC 37 Reflex check` save.
