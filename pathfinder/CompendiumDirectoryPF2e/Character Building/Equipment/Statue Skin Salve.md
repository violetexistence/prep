# Statue Skin Salve
## Item 5
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[oil]]

**Category** other; 
**Price** 25 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** held-in-one-hand

**Activate** `pf2:3` Interact

* * *

This gritty salve can be applied to the skin of a creature to form a thin layer of smooth stone on its body for 20 minutes. During this time, it gains resistance 3 to piercing and slashing damage. Further, the creature gains a +2 item bonus to [[Actions/Impersonate|Impersonate]] a statue or creature made of stone for 8 hours, or until its body is fully submersed in water.
