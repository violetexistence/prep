# Gliding Membranes (Greater)
## Item 7
### [[graft]][[invested]][[magical]]


**Price** 340 gp; 
**Bulk** L; **Size** med
**Usage** implanted

Membranes that stretch between your arms and torso help you convert a fall into a glide. Treat falls as 50 feet shorter. Even if you take fall damage, you can land on your feet by succeeding at a `DC 15 Acrobatics check` check.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
