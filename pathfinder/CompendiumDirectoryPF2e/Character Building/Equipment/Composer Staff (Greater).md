# Composer Staff (Greater)

### [[magical]][[staff]][[two-hand-d8]]

| **Category** simple melee weapon | **Group** club |

A _composer staff_ is short and shaped like an elegant black conductor's baton with a silver tip. When waved through the air, it hums melodically. In this way, you can play it as though it were an instrument, and it grants a +1 item bonus to Performance checks made with it.

* * *

**Activate** Cast a Spell

**Effect** You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.

*   **Cantrip** [[Spells/Haunting Hymn|Haunting Hymn]], [[Spells/Summon Instrument|Summon Instrument]]
*   **1st** [[Spells/Concordant Choir|Concordant Choir]]
*   **2nd** [[Spells/Sonata Span|Sonata Span]]
*   **3rd** [[Spells/Concordant Choir|Concordant Choir]], [[Spells/Enthrall|Enthrall]], [[Spells/Roaring Applause|Roaring Applause]]

* * *

**Craft Requirements** Supply one casting of all listed ranks of all listed spells.
