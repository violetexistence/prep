# Reflecting Shard (Greater)
## Item 17
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[consumable]][[magical]][[talisman]]

**Category** talisman; 
**Price** 2500 gp; 
**Bulk** 0; **Size** med
**Usage** affixed-to-a-shield

**Activate** `pf2:r` (concentrate)

**Trigger** You are targeted by a spell of any rank.

**Requirements** You're a master in Athletics, and you have the affixed shield raised.

* * *

This mirrored metal fragment is bolted or welded to the face of the affixed shield. When you Activate it, you attempt to reflect the triggering spell back at its caster with [[Spells/Spell Riposte|Spell Riposte]], using your `Athletics check` modifier for the counteract check. The talisman's counteract rank is 9th.

*Source: Pathfinder Treasure Vault (Remastered)*
