# Raconteur's Reload `pf2:1`

### [[gunslinger]]

**Category** offensive; 




Your rapid or forceful words draw the enemy's attention away from your hands long enough to chamber another bullet. Interact to reload and then attempt a Deception check to [[Actions/create-a-diversion variant=distracting-words|create-a-diversion variant=distracting-words]]{Create a Diversion} or an Intimidation check to [[Actions/demoralize|demoralize]].
