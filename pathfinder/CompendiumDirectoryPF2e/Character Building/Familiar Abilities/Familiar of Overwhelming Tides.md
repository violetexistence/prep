# Familiar of Overwhelming Tides

### 

**Category** familiar; 




Your familiar constantly drips with ocean water. When you Cast or Sustain a hex, you can cause a small wave to surge forth from your familiar. One creature within 10 feet of your familiar is pushed 5 feet away from your familiar.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
