# Jet

### 

**Category** familiar; 




Your familiar can use bursts of elemental energy or magic to [[Actions/Leap|Leap]] up to 30 feet in any direction, even if that would exceed its normal maximum distance for leaping. The Leap must begin and end on a solid surface capable of supporting the familiar.

*Source: Pathfinder Rage of Elements*
