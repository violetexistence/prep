# Major Creed
## Feat 13
### [[cleric]]

**Category** classfeature; 




You gain master proficiency with your deity's favored weapon. Your proficiency rank for Fortitude saves increases to master. When you roll a success on a Fortitude save, you get a critical success instead.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Divine Mysteries*
