# Lesser Creed
## Feat 5
### [[cleric]]

**Category** classfeature; 




You gain expert proficiency with your deity's favored weapon, martial weapons, simple weapons, and unarmed attacks. When you critically succeed at an attack roll using your deity's favored weapon, you apply the weapon's critical specialization effect. Your proficiency rank for your class DC increases to expert.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Divine Mysteries*
