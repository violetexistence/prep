# Shadow's Assault
## Feat 13
### [[fetchling]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Your shadow assaults your foes. You can cast [[Spells/Shadow Blast|Shadow Blast]] as a 5th-rank occult innate spell once per day. At 17th level, your _shadow blast_ is heightened to 6th level.
