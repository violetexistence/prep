# Conflux Focus
## Feat 12
### [[magus]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** conflux spells
* * *
You imagine how you'll weave magic and combat into future battles, improving your focus.

If you have spent at least 2 Focus Points since the last time you [[Actions/Refocus|Refocused]], you recover 2 Focus Points when you Refocus instead of 1.
