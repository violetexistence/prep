# Earthsense
## Feat 9
### [[oread]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Your connection to earth and stone allows you to feel the slightest displacement that disturbs them, down to the shift of a pebble. You gain tremorsense as an imprecise sense with a range of 30 feet.
