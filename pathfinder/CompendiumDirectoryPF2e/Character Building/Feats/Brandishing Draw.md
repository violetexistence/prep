# Brandishing Draw `pf2:1`
## Feat 2
### [[swashbuckler]]

**Category** class; 




You draw and attack with the same motion. You Interact to draw a weapon, then either Strike or use a one-action finisher. You must still meet any other requirements to use a finisher, and any Strike you make must be made with the weapon you drew.
