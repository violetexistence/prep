# Lengthy Diversion
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Deception
* * *
When you critically succeed to [[Actions/Create a Diversion|Create a Diversion]], you continue to remain [[Conditions/Hidden|Hidden]] after the end of your turn. This effect lasts for an amount of time that depends on the diversion and situation, as determined by the GM (minimum 1 additional round).
