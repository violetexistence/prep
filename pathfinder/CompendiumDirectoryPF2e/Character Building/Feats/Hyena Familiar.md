# Hyena Familiar
## Feat 1
### [[gnoll]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Hyenas serve kholo as pets and trackers. Some kholos, such as yourself, draw the attention of smaller hyenas that are vessels for magical spirits. You gain a Tiny hyena as a familiar.
