# Positioning Assault `pf2:2`
## Feat 8
### [[fighter]][[flourish]]

**Category** class; 




**Requirements** You are wielding a two-handed melee weapon and your target is within your reach.

* * *

With punishing blows, you force your opponent into position. Make a Strike with the required weapon. If you hit, you [[Actions/Reposition|Reposition]] the target 5 feet.
