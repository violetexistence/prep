# Impaling Bone
## Feat 17
### [[gnoll]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Once per day, you can cast [[Spells/Impaling Spike|Impaling Spike]] as a 7th-rank innate occult spell, except the spike is made of bone instead of cold iron. The bone from the spell has the effects of the _ghost touch_ property rune and can [[Conditions/Immobilized|Immobilize]] incorporeal creatures as if they were corporeal.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
