# Boost Summons
## Feat 8
### [[summoner]]

**Category** class; 




Augmenting your eidolon extends to creatures you summon. When you cast [[Spells/Boost Eidolon|Boost Eidolon]], in addition to your eidolon, it also targets your summoned creatures within 60 feet.
