# Alchemical Assessment
## Feat 1
### [[alchemist]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Crafting
* * *
You can identify alchemical items quickly. When using the Crafting skill to [[Actions/Identify Alchemy|Identify Alchemy]] on an alchemical item you hold, you can do so as a single action, rather than spending 10 minutes.
