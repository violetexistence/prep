# Cunning Climber
## Feat 9
### [[halfling]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Whether you are climbing a ship's rigging, a jungle tree, or a clock tower, you have an uncanny knack for finding footholds and handholds where larger creatures can't. You gain a climb Speed of 10 feet.
