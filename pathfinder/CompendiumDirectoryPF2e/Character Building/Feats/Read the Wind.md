# Read the Wind
## Feat 4
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[mythic]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Hunter's Calling
* * *
You can derive meaning from the tiniest details when tracking an enemy. Whenever you succeed at a check to [[Actions/Track|Track]] at mythic proficiency, you can immediately attempt a check to Recall Knowledge about the creature you're tracking, also at mythic proficiency and with a suitable skill determined by the GM.

*Source: Pathfinder War of Immortals*
