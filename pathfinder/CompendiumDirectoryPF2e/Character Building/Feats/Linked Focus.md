# Linked Focus
## Feat 4
### [[wizard]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** arcane bond; curriculum spells
* * *
You have linked your bonded item to the well of energy that powers your school spells. Once per day when you [[Actions/Drain Bonded Item|Drain your Bonded Item]] to cast one of your arcane school's curriculum spells, you also regain 1 Focus Point.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
