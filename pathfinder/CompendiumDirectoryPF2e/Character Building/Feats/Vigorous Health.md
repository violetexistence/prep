# Vigorous Health
## Feat 1
### [[hobgoblin]]

**Category** ancestry; 




You can withstand blood loss startlingly well. Whenever you would gain the [[Conditions/Drained|Drained]] condition, you can attempt a `DC 17 Flat check`. On a success, you don't gain the drained condition.
