# Luck of the Clowder
## Feat 9
### [[catfolk]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Cat's Luck
* * *
When you use [[Feats/Cat's Luck|Cat's Luck]] to reroll, any creatures you choose within 10 feet who also just failed against the same effect (such as a single spell) can also reroll their saving throws and use the better result.
