# Harmlessly Cute
## Feat 1
### [[leshy]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Your size and demeanor make it easy for you to convince others that you mean no harm. You gain the [[Feats/Shameless Request|Shameless Request]] skill feat. Additionally, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to initiative checks when you roll Deception for initiative.
