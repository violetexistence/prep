# Handy with your Paws
## Feat 1
### [[shoony]]

**Category** ancestry; 




You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Crafting checks to [[Actions/Repair|Repair]] non-magical items, and you don't need a [[Equipment/Repair Toolkit|Repair Kit]] for such repairs as long as you have any amount of junk, spare parts, or debris at your disposal.

*Source: Pathfinder #153: Life's Long Shadows*
