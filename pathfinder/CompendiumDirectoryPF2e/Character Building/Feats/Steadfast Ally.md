# Steadfast Ally
## Feat 13
### [[shoony]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Loyal Empath
* * *
Your presence alone is enough to provide your allies with emotional support. You can use the [[Actions/Aid|Aid]] reaction to grant a bonus to another creature's Will saving throw even when you haven't prepared to help.
