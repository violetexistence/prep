# Hard Tail
## Feat 1
### [[goblin]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** tailed goblin heritage
* * *
Your tail is much stronger than most, and you can lash out with it with the strength of a whip. You gain a tail unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage.
