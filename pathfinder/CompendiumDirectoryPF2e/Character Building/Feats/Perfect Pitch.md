# Perfect Pitch
## Feat 12
### [uncommon](uncommon "Uncommon Rarity Trait")[[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Celebrity or Dandy Dedication
* * *
Your ear for harmony has enhanced your ability with musical magic. You can use Performance to [[Actions/Learn a Spell|Learn a Spell]] or [[Actions/Identify Magic|Identify Magic]] spells and effects that are musical in nature. In addition, you gain the [[Actions/Pitch-Perfect Projection|Pitch-Perfect Projection]] action.

*Source: Pathfinder #204: Stage Fright*
