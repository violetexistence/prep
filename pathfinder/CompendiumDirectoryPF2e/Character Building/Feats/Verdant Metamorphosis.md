# Verdant Metamorphosis
## Feat 14
### [[druid]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** leaf order
* * *
You have turned into a plant version of yourself, gaining the plant trait and losing traits inappropriate for your new form (typically humanoid). You also gain the [[Actions/Verdant Rest|Verdant Rest]] action.
