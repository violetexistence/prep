# Phantom Charm `pf2:0`
## Feat 13
### [[divine]][[fortune]][[minotaur]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** ghost bull minotaur heritage
* * *
**Frequency** once per day

**Trigger** You attempt a check that would be subject to a misfortune effect.

* * *
