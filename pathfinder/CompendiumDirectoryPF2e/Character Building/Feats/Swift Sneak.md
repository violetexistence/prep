# Swift Sneak
## Feat 7
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** master in Stealth
* * *
You can move your full Speed when you [[Actions/Sneak|Sneak]]. You can use Swift Sneak while Burrowing, Climbing, Flying, or Swimming instead of Striding if you have the corresponding movement type.
