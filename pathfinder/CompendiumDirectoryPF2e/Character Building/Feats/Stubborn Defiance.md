# Stubborn Defiance
## Feat 9
### [[centaur]]

**Category** ancestry; 




You're brave and stubborn and refuse to back down or bow to the wishes of another. You gain a +1 status bonus to saves against mental effects. Additionally, you gain the [[Actions/I Defy You!|I Defy You!]] reaction.
