# Cremate Undead
## Feat 8
### [[cleric]]

**Category** class; 




Your overwhelming vitality sets undead alight. When you use a [[Spells/Heal|Heal]] spell to damage undead, each undead that takes damage also takes persistent fire damage equal to the spell's rank.
