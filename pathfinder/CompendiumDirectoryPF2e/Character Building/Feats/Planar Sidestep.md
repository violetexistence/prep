## Feat 13
### [[arcane]][[ardande]][[concentrate]][[naari]][[oread]][[suli]][[sylph]][[talos]][[undine]]

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per day

**Trigger** A creature hits you with a melee Strike.

* * *
