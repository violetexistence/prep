# Companion's Cry
## Feat 4
### [[ranger]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** an animal companion
* * *
You can urge your companion to do its utmost. You can spend 2 actions to [[Actions/Command an Animal|Command an Animal]] instead of 1 when commanding your animal companion. If you do, your animal companion uses an additional action.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
