# Quick Repair
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Crafting
* * *
For you, the [[Actions/Repair|Repair]] activity loses the exploration trait and takes 1 minute instead of 10 minutes. If you're a master in Crafting, it takes 3 actions. If you're legendary, it takes 1 action.
