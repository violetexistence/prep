# Growing Eel Friend
## Feat 5
### [[athamaru]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** Elver Pet
* * *
Your eel pet grows larger and stronger. Your eel pet's size changes to Small, and the eel gains the [[Familiar Abilities/Scent|Scent]] and [[Familiar Abilities/Tough|Tough]] pet abilities. However, it becomes too large to carry in a water-filled globe.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
