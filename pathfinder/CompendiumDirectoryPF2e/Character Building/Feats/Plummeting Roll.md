# Plummeting Roll
## Feat 1
### [[swashbuckler]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Acrobatics
* * *
Through nimble movements, a graceful landing, and a bit of luck, you can manage to fall from spectacular heights without a scratch. You gain the [[Feats/Cat Fall|Cat Fall]] skill feat and the [[Actions/Plummeting Roll|Plummeting Roll]] reaction.
