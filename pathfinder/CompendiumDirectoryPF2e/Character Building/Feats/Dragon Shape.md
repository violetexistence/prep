# Dragon Shape
## Feat 12
### [[druid]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Soaring Shape
* * *
You can take on the shape of some of the world's most fearsome creatures. Add the shapes listed in [[Spells/Dragon Form|Dragon Form]] to your [[Spells/Untamed Form|Untamed Form]] list. Whenever you're polymorphed into another form using _untamed form_, you gain resistance 5 to your choice of bludgeoning or poison.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
