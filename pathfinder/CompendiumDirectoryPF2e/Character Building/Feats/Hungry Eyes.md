# Hungry Eyes
## Feat 1
### [[hungerseed]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Your eyes can see through darkness with an oni's visual acuity. You gain low-light vision, or you gain darkvision if you already have low-light vision.

* * *

**Special** You can select this feat twice, once to gain low-light vision and a second time to gain darkvision.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Tian Xia Character Guide*
