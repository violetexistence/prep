# Impossible Snares
## Feat 20
### [[ranger]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Ubiquitous Snares
* * *
You can create endless shorter-lived snares, protecting your lair or using them against foes.

Once per minute, you can [[Actions/Craft|Craft]] one of the snares you've prepared for quick deployment without expending the prepared snare. Once deployed, such a snare lasts for 10 minutes before losing its effectiveness.

*Source: Pathfinder Advanced Player's Guide*
