# Evade Doom
## Feat 9
### [[catfolk]]

**Category** ancestry; 




You can elude supernatural disaster. When you would gain the [[Conditions/Doomed|Doomed]] condition, you can attempt a `DC 17 Flat check` check. On a success, you don't gain the doomed condition.
