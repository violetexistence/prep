# Quick Jump
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Athletics
* * *
You can use [[Actions/High Jump|High Jump]] and [[Actions/Long Jump|Long Jump]] as a single action instead of 2 actions. If you do, you don't perform the initial Stride (nor do you fail if you don't Stride 10 feet).
