# Vibration Sense
## Feat 4
### [[evolution]][[summoner]]

**Category** class; 




Your eidolon gains [[Bestiary Ability Glossary/Tremorsense|Tremorsense]] as an imprecise sense with a range of 30 feet. If aquatic, it gains [[Bestiary Ability Glossary/Wavesense|Wavesense]] as an imprecise sense with a range of 30 feet instead. If amphibious, it gains both.
