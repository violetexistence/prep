# Basic Cathartic Spellcasting
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Cathartic Mage Dedication
* * *
You gain the [[PF2e Journals/Archetypes/Basic Spellcasting Feat|Basic Spellcasting Feat]] benefits. Each time you gain a spell slot of a new rank from this archetype, add a spell of that spell rank from your chosen tradition to your repertoire-either a common spell or another spell you've learned or discovered.
