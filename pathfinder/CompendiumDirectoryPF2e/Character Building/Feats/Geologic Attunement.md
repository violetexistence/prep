# Geologic Attunement `pf2:1`
## Feat 1
### [[earth]][[impulse]][[kineticist]][[primal]][[stance]]

**Category** class; 




You tune to the earth and stone around you, extending your senses through it in rippling waves. You gain tremorsense (imprecise) in your kinetic aura. Once per round, you can [[Actions/Point Out|Point Out]] a creature you can sense with tremorsense as a free action.

[[Feat_Feature Effects/Stance_ Geologic Attunement|Stance: Geologic Attunement]]

* * *

**Level (13th)** The tremorsense becomes precise.
