# Crocodile's Twin
## Feat 1
### [[lizardfolk]]

**Category** ancestry; 



**Prerequisites** bakuwa lizardfolk heritage or makari lizardfolk heritage
* * *
Your egg contained your twin, a crocodile. Considered a rare and auspicious event, you share a magical bond with this sibling. You gain a tiny crocodile as a familiar.

* * *

**Special** You can take this feat only at 1st level.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Tian Xia Character Guide*
