# Tumbling Teamwork
## Feat 2
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** expert in Acrobatics
* * *
Your tumbling distracts a foe enough to create an advantage for one of your allies. When you successfully [[Actions/Tumble Through|Tumble Through]] an enemy's space, an ally who is adjacent to that enemy can Step as a reaction, but they must remain adjacent to that enemy.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
