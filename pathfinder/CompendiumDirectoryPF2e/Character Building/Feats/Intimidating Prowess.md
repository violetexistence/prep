# Intimidating Prowess
## Feat 2
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** Strength +3; expert in Intimidation
* * *
In situations where you can physically menace the target when you [[Actions/Coerce|Coerce]] or [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]], you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your Intimidation check and you ignore the penalty for not sharing a language. If your Strength modifier is +5 or higher and you are a master in Intimidation, this bonus increases to +2.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
