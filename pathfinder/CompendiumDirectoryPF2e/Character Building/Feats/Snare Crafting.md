# Snare Crafting
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Crafting
* * *
You can use the Craft activity to create snares. When you select this feat, you add the formulas for four common 1st-level snares to your formula book.
