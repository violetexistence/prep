# Battle Cry
## Feat 7
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** master in Intimidation
* * *
When you roll initiative, you can yell a mighty battle cry and [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]] an observed foe as a free action. If you're legendary in Intimidation, you can use a reaction to Demoralize your foe when you critically succeed at an attack roll.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core*
