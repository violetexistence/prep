# Improved Twin Riposte (Fighter)
## Feat 14
### [[fighter]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Twin Riposte
* * *
Your weapons are a blur, blocking and biting at your foes. You can use [[Feats/Twin Riposte|Twin Riposte]] even if you aren't benefiting from [[Feats/Twin Parry|Twin Parry]]. At the start of each of your turns, you gain an additional reaction that you can use only to make a Twin Riposte.
