# Powerful Leap
## Feat 2
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** expert in Athletics
* * *
You can jump 5 feet up with a vertical [[Actions/Leap|Leap]] without making a [[Actions/High Jump|High Jump]]. You also increase the horizontal distance when you Leap, including as part of a High Jump or [[Actions/Long Jump|Long Jump]], by 5 feet.
