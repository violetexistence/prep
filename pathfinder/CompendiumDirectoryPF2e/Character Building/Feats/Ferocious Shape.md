# Ferocious Shape
## Feat 8
### [[druid]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Untamed Form
* * *
You've mastered the shape of dinosaurs. Add the shapes listed in [[Spells/Dinosaur Form|Dinosaur Form]] to your untamed form list. Whenever you use [[Spells/Untamed Form|Untamed Form]] to take a shape that grants you a specific Athletics modifier, you gain a +1 status bonus to your Athletics checks.
