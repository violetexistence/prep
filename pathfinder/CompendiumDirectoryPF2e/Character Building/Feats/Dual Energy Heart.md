# Dual Energy Heart
## Feat 4
### [[evolution]][[summoner]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Energy Heart
* * *
A second energy dwells in your eidolon. Choose an additional energy damage type. Your eidolon's unarmed attack that deals energy damage gains the versatile trait for the second energy damage type, and your eidolon gains resistance to the second type of energy damage equal to half your level.

*Source: Pathfinder Secrets of Magic*
