# Lion's Magic
## Feat 8
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Lion Blade Dedication
* * *
Many Lion Blades enhance their words with a bit of magic. You can cast a 4th-rank [[Spells/Suggestion|Suggestion]] as an occult innate spell once per day. At 12th level, you can also cast a 5th-rank [[Spells/Subconscious Suggestion|Subconscious Suggestion]] once per day.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Shining Kingdoms*
