# Quick Coercion
## Feat 1
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** trained in Intimidation
* * *
You can bully others with just a few choice implications. You can [[Actions/Coerce|Coerce]] a creature after 1 round of conversation instead of 1 minute. You still can't Coerce a creature in the midst of combat, or without engaging in a conversation.
