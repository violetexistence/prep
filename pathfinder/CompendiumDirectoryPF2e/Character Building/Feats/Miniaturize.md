# Miniaturize
## Feat 8
### [[evolution]][[summoner]]

**Category** class;

Your eidolon can shrink even further. When using [[Feats/Shrink Down|Shrink Down]], your eidolon can reduce its size to Tiny. When Tiny, its reach is 0 feet.
