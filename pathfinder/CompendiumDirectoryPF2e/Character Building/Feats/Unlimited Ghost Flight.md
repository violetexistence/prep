# Unlimited Ghost Flight
## Feat 14
### [[archetype]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** Ghost Flight
* * *
You put your connection to the material world farther behind you. Your fly Speed no longer restricts the height you can fly. When you use [[Feats/Ghost Flight|Ghost Flight]], instead of its normal effect, you gain a +10-foot status bonus to your fly Speed for 10 minutes.
