# Dragon's Flight `pf2:1`
## Feat 5
### [[dragonblood]]

**Category** ancestry; 




**Frequency** once per round

* * *

You have grown a small pair of draconic wings or have honed your use of the wings you've had since birth. You Fly. If you don't normally have a fly Speed, you gain a fly Speed of 20 feet for this movement. If you aren't on solid ground at the end of this movement, you fall.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
