# Inspired Stratagem
## Feat 8
### [[rogue]]

**Category** class; 




During your daily preparations, you can review stratagems for the day's adventures with up to five allies. Later, you can quickly advise them on your schemes using the [[Actions/Inspired Stratagem|Inspired Stratagem]] reaction.
