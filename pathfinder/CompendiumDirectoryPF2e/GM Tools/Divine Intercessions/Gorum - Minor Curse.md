# Gorum - Minor Curse
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** curse; 




Gorum rewards cowardice with frailty. Any armor you wear and shield you wield reduces its item bonus to AC by 2 (minimum 0) and its Hardness, Hit Points, and Break Threshold by half.
