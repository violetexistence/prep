# Shelyn - Minor Boon
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** deityboon; 




Once, when you roll a failure on a Diplomacy check, you get a critical success instead.

Shelyn typically grants this boon only when the Diplomacy check would serve to increase love or offer a chance of redemption.
