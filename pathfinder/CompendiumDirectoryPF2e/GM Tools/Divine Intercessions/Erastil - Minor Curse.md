# Erastil - Minor Curse
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** curse; 




Erastil curses your table and punishes you for relying on others' hard work. Any food you didn't grow, gather, or otherwise harvest yourself tastes like ash and leaves you [[Conditions/Fatigued|Fatigued]].
