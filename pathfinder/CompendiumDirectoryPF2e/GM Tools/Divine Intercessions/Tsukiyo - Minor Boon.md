# Tsukiyo - Minor Boon

### 






Tsukiyo's simplest show of gratitude is a gift of clarity.

Once, when you roll a failure on a saving throw against a mental effect, you get a critical success instead.

Tsukiyo typically grants this boon against a particularly consequential mental effect.
