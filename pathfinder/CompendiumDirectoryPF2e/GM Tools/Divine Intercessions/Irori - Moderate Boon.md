# Irori - Moderate Boon
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** deityboon; 




Your body rebuilds after adversity, becoming stronger. You can cast [[Spells/Harmonize Self|Harmonize Self]] as an occult ki spell. If you didn't have one already, you gain a focus pool with 1 Focus Point and are trained in occult spell attack rolls and spell DCs.
