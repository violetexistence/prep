# Nethys - Minor Boon
## Feat 1
### 

**Category** deityboon; 




Nethys casts a powerful spell to help you in a pinch.

Once, Nethys casts a spell 1 rank higher than the highest-rank spell you can cast. If you can't cast spells, Nethys casts a 1st-rank spell to help you.
