# (Martial Artist, Dirty Fighting) Illegal Bite `pf2:1`

### 

**Category** offensive; 




The martial artist makes an `Athletics check` check against a target's Fortitude DC to bite them in a vulnerable area. On a success, the martial artist deals 2d6 piercing damage (or double on a critical success).
