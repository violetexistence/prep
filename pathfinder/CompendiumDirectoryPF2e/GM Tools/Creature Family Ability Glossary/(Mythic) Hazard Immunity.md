# (Mythic) Hazard Immunity

### 

**Category** defensive; 




A mythic ambusher never triggers the reactions of hazards in its own lair and is immune to the negative consequences of damaging area effects created by hazards in its lair.

*Source: Pathfinder War of Immortals*
