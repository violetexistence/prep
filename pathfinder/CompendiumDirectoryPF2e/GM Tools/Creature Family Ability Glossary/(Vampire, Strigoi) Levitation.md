# (Vampire, Strigoi) Levitation `pf2:2`

### [[divine]][[shadow]]

**Category** defensive; 




Strigoi can cast [[Spells/Levitate|Levitate]] at will as a divine innate spell; when they do so, they appear to ascend or descend on a coiling mass of shadows.
