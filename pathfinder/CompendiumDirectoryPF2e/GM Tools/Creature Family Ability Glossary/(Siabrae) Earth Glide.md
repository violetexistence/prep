# (Siabrae) Earth Glide

### 

**Category** offensive; 




The siabrae can [[Actions/Burrow|Burrow]] through any earthen matter, including rock. When it does so, the siabrae moves at its full burrow Speed, leaving no tunnels or signs of its passing.

*Source: Pathfinder Book of the Dead*
