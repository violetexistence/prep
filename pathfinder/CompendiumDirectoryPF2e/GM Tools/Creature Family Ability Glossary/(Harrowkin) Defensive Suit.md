# (Harrowkin) Defensive Suit

### 

**Category** offensive; 




When the harrowkin attempts a saving throw connected to its suit—that is, Fortitude (Hammers or Shields), Reflex (Keys or Books), or Will (Stars or Crowns)—if they roll a success, they get a critical success instead.

*Source: Pathfinder #192: Worst of All Possible Worlds*
