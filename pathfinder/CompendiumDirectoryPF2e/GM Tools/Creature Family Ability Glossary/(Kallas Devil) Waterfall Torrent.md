# (Kallas Devil) Waterfall Torrent

### 

**Category** offensive; 




The Kallas Devil controls any water she touches. When the Kallas Devil is in water, she can cast [[Spells/Hydraulic Push|Hydraulic Push]] as an occult innate spell at will.
