# (Mythic) Deadly Striker

### 

**Category** offensive; 




A mythic striker deals an additional 1d6 precision damage on its next Strike whenever it uses an action to Stride 10 or more feet (or to Burrow, Climb, or Fly 10 or more feet).

*Source: Pathfinder War of Immortals*
