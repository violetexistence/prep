# (Failed Prophet) Wealthsense

### 

**Category** interaction; 




The failed prophet can detect concentrations of coinage and other valuables as an imprecise sense with a range of 60 feet. The failed prophet detects any creature carrying coins and treasure totaling at least the average cost of a consumable item of the failed prophet's level.

*Source: Pathfinder Lost Omens Shining Kingdoms*
