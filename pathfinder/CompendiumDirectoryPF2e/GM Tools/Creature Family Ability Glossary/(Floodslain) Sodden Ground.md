# (Floodslain) Sodden Ground

### [[aura]][[occult]][[water]]

**Category** offensive; 




20 feet. Water flows endlessly from a floodslain creature, making the area around it slippery. The ground in the aura is difficult terrain for all non-floodslain creatures.
