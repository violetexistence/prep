# Effect: Silvertongue Mutagen (Moderate)
## Level 3
### 






Granted by [[Equipment/Silvertongue Mutagen (Moderate)|Silvertongue Mutagen (Moderate)]]

**Benefit** You gain a +2 item bonus to Deception, Diplomacy, Intimidation and Performance checks. Your critical failures with any of these skills become failures instead.

**Drawback** You take a -2 item penalty to Arcana, Crafting, Lore, Occultism, and Society checks. Choose one skill in which you are trained; for the duration, you become untrained in that skill. All your failures to Recall Knowledge become critical failures.

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
